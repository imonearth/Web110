Leonardo da Vinci: Mona Lisa, oil on wood panel by Leonardo da Vinci, c. 1503 to 1519; in the Louvre, Paris.

Can you imagine this original hanging on the mantle above your fireplace? The Mona Lisa painted against a distant landscape, with a stoic expression and life like features, was at the center of the Renaissance period in art lives in Paris at the Louvre.
